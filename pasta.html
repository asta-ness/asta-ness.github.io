making fresh pasta

Ingredients

  • 500g flour
  • 5 eggs

Metods

  1. Sift the flour onto a works surface and forming it into "a volcano"-shaped mound with a well in the centre.
  2. Break the eggs into this well.
  3. Incorporate the eggs into the flour with a fork and your hands. Gradually drawing the flour into the eggs mixture until it forms a coarse paste.
  4. Knead dough, work the dough for 10-15 minutes until the consistency is smooth and elastic.
  5. Wrap the dough in clingfilm and leave it to rest for at least half an hour.
